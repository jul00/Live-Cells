# Live Cells (working title)
A rougelike dungeon-diving platformer with a platform fighter kick.

## Core Gameplay Loop
- Fight through rooms filled with enemies within dungeons.
- Get boons to improve your moveset and heal before getting back into the fight.
- Face a boss at the end of each dungeon until you reach the final boss. 
- Get legendary boon from boss.
- Reset from nothing if you die.

## Genre & Inspirations
Platformer
Dead Cells meets Smash Bros.

## Technical Feasibility
Systems:
- Dungeon Generation
- Enemy AI
- Combat Interaction

Biggest Technical Risk: 
Logic checks for for interactions might cause stutter.

Week 5 Feasibility Validation:
Working player moveset and combat. 

## Scope Management
Minimum Viable Product:
Core gameplay loop of fighting through dungeons including combat, movement, boon system, UI, and at least 2 bossfights.

Stretch Goals:
All four bossfights and dungeons.
